Deforestation: Losing Earth's Lungs

Forests are being cleared at an alarming rate for agriculture, urban expansion, and timber. This not only destroys habitats but also contributes to climate change.

🌳 Habitat Destruction

Millions of species lose their homes when forests are cleared, pushing them toward extinction.

🌫 More Carbon, Less Oxygen

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen. Cutting them increases greenhouse gases in the air.

🌧 Weather Disruption

Forests regulate rainfall. Their destruction causes floods, droughts, and imbalance in local climates.
