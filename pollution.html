Pollution: Poisoning Our Planet

Pollution is one of the greatest threats to Earth’s ecosystems. Human activities are releasing harmful substances into the air, water, and soil at alarming rates.

🌫 Air Pollution

Emissions from vehicles, industries, and burning fossil fuels contribute to smog, acid rain, and respiratory diseases.

💧 Water Pollution

Plastic waste, oil spills, and chemical discharge are destroying aquatic life and contaminating drinking water.

🌾 Soil Pollution

Excessive pesticide use, landfills, and waste dumping degrade soil quality, affecting crops and food safety.
