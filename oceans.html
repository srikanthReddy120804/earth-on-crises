Oceans in Crisis: Drowning in Danger

Oceans cover over 70% of the Earth and support an incredible variety of life. But they are being polluted and overexploited at a rapid pace.

🛢 Oil Spills & Industrial Waste

Spills and runoff contaminate marine environments and kill aquatic species.

🪸 Coral Bleaching

Rising sea temperatures and acidification are destroying coral reefs — the rainforests of the sea.

🧴 Plastic Pollution

Millions of tons of plastic end up in the ocean each year, harming marine animals and ecosystems.
