Natural Disasters: Nature’s Response

Natural disasters are becoming more frequent and severe due to human influence on the environment. Climate change, deforestation, and pollution have all intensified these events.

🌪 Storms & Hurricanes

Warmer oceans fuel stronger storms, displacing communities and damaging ecosystems.

🔥 Wildfires

Rising temperatures and dry conditions lead to massive forest fires, releasing tons of CO₂.

🌊 Floods & Droughts

Unpredictable rainfall patterns cause destructive flooding in some regions and drought in others.
